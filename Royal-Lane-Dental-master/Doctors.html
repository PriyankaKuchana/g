    Meet Our Royal Lane Dental Center Doctor

    Deepa Kastoori, DDS

    Dr. Kastoori is thrilled to be serving the Dallas community and growing the Royal Lane Dental Center family!

    As a child, Dr. Kastoori grew up watching many of her family members in various areas of the medical field improve the health of their community. In high school, she decided that pursuing a career in dentistry would be the perfect way for her to do the same. Dr. Kastoori began her dental education in India and received her Bachelor of Dental Surgery degree there. She then moved to the U.S. and completed her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree at the NYU College of Dentistry in New York City.

    After earning her Doctorate, Dr. Kastoori completed her externships at the VA Medical Center in Manhattan. She stays up to date on the latest advancements in dentistry by taking continuing education courses on topics like Invisalign, dental implants, and surgical extractions. Dr. Kastoori knew she wanted to own a dental office before she even applied to dental school, as she believes owning her own practice allows her to practice dentistry to the best of her abilities. Working at Royal Lane Dental Center is a dream come true for her, and she is excited to work with both new and returning patients alike.

    Dr. Kastoori loves being able to help patients not only treat their dental issues but enhance their overall appearance as well. She finds helping patients improve their confidence and the quality of their lives to be very rewarding. Dr. Kastoori is married and has two children. Outside of the office, she enjoys spending time with family and friends and traveling. She looks forward to meeting you!
