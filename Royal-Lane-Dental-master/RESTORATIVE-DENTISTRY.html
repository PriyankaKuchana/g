    Full & Partial
    Dentures

    Dentures are a prosthesis composed of a pink, gum-like structure with false teeth. Full dentures are used to replace a full row of teeth, while partial dentures are used to replace just a few. Dentures aid in maintaining facial structure and give you the ability to chew and speak as you would with a full set of teeth.

    Dental Implants

    Implants may serve as a base for a dental crown, dentures, or dental implants. These small, titanium posts are surgically fused with your jawbone to act as an artificial tooth root. Once your jawbone has healed, a connective structure called an abutment will be attached and your restorative piece can be placed. While the cost of dental implants can be a bit higher than other restorations, they are customized for your mouth and can last a lifetime with proper care.

    Dental Crowns

    With the ability to improve both the appearance and functionality of your smile, dental crowns are a popular treatment method in restorative dentistry. A crown is a tooth-shaped cap that is permanently bonded to your natural tooth or connected to a dental implant. Our team will take impressions and send them to an off-site lab, where your custom piece will be created. Once we have received it, we will permanently attach it to your tooth or implant and inspect the fit, shade, and shape. With proper care, dental crowns can last up to 15 years or longer.

    Contact our team today for more information or to schedule a consultation to determine if dental crowns are a good option for you.

    Root Canal Therapy

    Root canal therapy can save a tooth from extraction and alleviate pain. During this procedure, we will carefully remove the infected pulp, clean the root canals, and use a filling to seal the area once more. Though many people are intimidated by this procedure, it is extremely common and has a very high success rate. Our skilled team will work diligently to keep you comfortable throughout your treatment.
